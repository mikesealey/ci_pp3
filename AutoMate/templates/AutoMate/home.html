Welcome to AutoMate – Your Trusted Car Service & Repair Booking Platform

At AutoMate, we make car servicing and repairs simple, fast, and hassle-free. Whether your vehicle needs a routine service, an urgent repair, or a complete check-up, our platform allows you to book an appointment with one of our trusted mechanics in just a few clicks.

How It Works

  1. Register for an account and provide your contact details
  2. Enter Your Vehicle Details – Provide your car’s registration number so we can tailor our services to your needs.
  3. Choose Your Service – Select from MOTs, general servicing, repair, diagnostics, and more. Need a repair? No problem—let us know the issue.
  4. Pick a Date & Time – Choose a convenient appointment slot that fits your schedule.
  5. Confirm Your Booking – Our mechanics will send you confirmation, along with details of your booking and service location.

Why choose AutoMate?

  • Easy Online Booking – No need for phone calls or long wait times. Book at your convenience.
  • Trusted Local Mechanics – We recruit and develop the finest automotive engineers to ensure top-quality service.
  • Reminders & Updates – Stay informed with appointment reminders and real-time updates.
